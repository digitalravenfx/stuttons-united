← Friday, February 12

Friday Events

We'd like to welcome our family and friends to experience the best parts of Leavenworth. Pick one, pick none, or invent your own. We'll share official sign up information when all of our guests have confirmed attendance and selected the activities that most interests them.
Please be sure to share your email with your RSVP.
Section one

Outdoor adventures.

01
Ski or snowboard

Downhill skiing or snowboarding at one of the two nearby resorts less than 35 minutes away from downtown Leavenworth. 

Mission Ridge ↗ Stevens Pass ↗
02
Cross-country skiing

Explore the Nordic trails at the nearby Nordic Center. Gear is available to rent so you can hit the trail at your own pace.

Stevens Pass Nordic ↗
03
Sledding

Laugh your way down the hill and be sure to bring your own sled for the adventure (no metal).

Stevens Pass ↗
04
Snow tubing

Multiple locations provide fast, easy runs close to town or a little further out (tubes provided).

Squilchuck State Park ↗
05
Snowshoeing

A guided snowshoe tour through the trails just outside the village. Gentle elevation, suitable for any fitness level.

Snowshoeing in Leavenworth ↗
06
Snowmobiling

Ride through the Lake Wenatchee snowmobile park with a local guide who's excited to show you their favorite trails. 

leavenworthsnowmobile.com ↗
Section two

Bring the kids.

07
Reindeer farm

Get up close and personal with some of Santa's finest. A short presentation, then a chance to feed and meet the reindeer.

leavenworthreindeer.com ↗
08
Adventure Park / Alpine Slide

A must when visiting Leavenworth. Climb to the top and slide down at whatever speed your heart desires.

leavenworthadventurepark.com ↗
Section three

Take it easy.

09
Nature park visit

Take a SnowCat to the top of the mountains and enjoy the best views in the PNW from a string of A-frame cabins in the backcountry.

Alpine Lakes Wilderness ↗
10
Wine tasting tour

A guided afternoon through the best wineries in town. The relaxed-Friday option.

Leavenworth wineries ↗
11
Spa & massage

Unwind at the resort's spa with a massage, facial, or soak — the warmest way to spend a snowy afternoon.

Sleeping Lady Spa ↗

Costs, timing, and sign-ups will be sent out once interest is confirmed this fall.
